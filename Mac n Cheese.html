Mac N' Cheese


Ingredients

  • 2 tbsps of butter
  • 2 tbsps of flour
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of hot milk
  • Salt and pepper seasoning
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheese
  • Macaroni

Description

Mac and cheese is a comfort food dish made with macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce. It is a known side dish in American cuisine, often served as an appetizer or main course. Mac and cheese is creamy and cheesy, and can satisfy cravings.


Procedure

  1. Over medium heat, put 2 tbsps of butter into the pan.
  2. Keeping the sauce nice and light, add 2 tbsps of flour.
  3. Turn the heat to low and stir constantly with a whisk until it is evenly mixed up.
  4. Once it starts to bubble, add 1 and 1/4 cups of hot milk.
  5. Turn up the heat and bring to a boil.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Once it has thickened up, turn off the heat.
  8. Add 1/2 cup of cheese and whisk until it all blends in.
  9. Add water if it gets too thick.
  10. Add boiled macaroni.
