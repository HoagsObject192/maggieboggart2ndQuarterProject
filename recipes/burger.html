Classic Burger


Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • Tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • Onions, thinly sliced
  • Cheese slices
  • Burger buns

Rating

Description

A juicy beef patty is paired with melted cheese, fresh lettuce, and ripe tomatoes inside a soft bun. Each bite balances tender meat, crisp veggies, and pillowy bread. The ultimate comfort food classic.


Procedure

    • Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
    • Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
    • Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix until well blended.
    • Form into four ¾‑inch‑thick patties.
    • Place patties on the preheated grill and cook 6–8 minutes per side, until cooked to desired doneness (160 °F internal temperature).
    • Layer on top of buns with tomatoes, cheese, lettuce, onions, and pickles (optional)
    • Serve hot and enjoy!
