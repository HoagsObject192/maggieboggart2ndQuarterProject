site creator: maggie tan

Maggie grew up in a family that LOVES cooking. Her grandma is a baker with her own bakeshop, and her dad, well, aside from being the franchiser of a fast food company, is also a huge food enthusiast. As you can imagine, not a week goes by without Maggie receiving freshly baked goods from her grandma or her dad experimenting on food for dinner. This inspired Maggie to get into culinary, specifically baking, and eventually she grew into a determined recipe collector. She wanted to find a way to present the recipes she has collected in a way that was more accessible given the present period, and this cs project gave her the oppurtunity to do so.
