Jeremiah Fisher's Dream Wedding Cake


Ingredients

    Raspberry Coulis

  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 3 tbsps of lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 cups of frozen raspberries

    • Chocolate Cake

  • 6 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup dutch proccess cocoa powder
  • 4 cups of white sugar
  • 4 tsps of baking soda
  • 2 tsps of salt
  • 4 cups of warm coffee
  • 4 tbsps of white vinegar
  • 2 tbsps of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/3 cups of vegetable oil

    • Mirror Glaze

  • 2/3 cup water
  • 2/3 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup + 2 tbsp. white sugar

Description

This cake shows how big of a red flag Jeremiah is. It is a towering multi-layered cake adorned with rich and glossy decorations, symbolizing his extravagant and over-the-top personality.


Procedure

    Raspberry Coulis

  1. Mix the sugar and lemon juice together and put it in a microwave
  2. Stir in 10-second increments.
  3. Blend the raspberries and the mixture until it is smooth.
  4. Pour the puree through a fine-mesh strainer over another bowl.
  5. Throw away the seeds and refrigerate the coulis until it will be used.

    6. Chocolate Cake

  6. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  7. grease and line two 8″ cake pans and two 6″ cake pans. To a large bowl, add in your dry ingredients and whisk until combined
  8. add in your dry ingredients and whisk until combined: 6 cups all-purpose flour, 1 cup dutch process cocoa powder, 4 cups white sugar, 4 tsp. baking soda, and 2 tsp. salt.
  9. Mix together wet ingredients.
  10. evenly distribute the batter into the 4 cake pans.
