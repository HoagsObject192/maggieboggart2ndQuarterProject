Caesar Salad


Ingredients

  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Description

Crisp romaine lettuce is tossed in a creamy, garlicky dressing with Parmesan and crunchy croutons. Each bite is fresh and savory, with a satisfying crunch. A classic salad that feels both light and indulgent.


Procedure

    • Gather all ingredients needed to make the classic Caesar salad.
    • In a large salad bowl, mash the anchovy fillets together with the garlic until smooth.
    • Add lemon juice, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, egg yolk, and Worcestershire sauce to the bowl.
    • Whisk the mixture until smooth and creamy.
    • Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking continuously to emulsify the dressing.
    • Season the dressing with salt and black pepper to taste.
    • Gently toss the romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into the dressing until evenly coated.
    • Top the salad with croutons.
    • Serve immediately and enjoy.
