Chicken Tacos


Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 cup lemonade
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 head lettuce, shredded
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream

Description

Tender, seasoned chicken is tucked into warm tortillas with fresh toppings like salsa and cilantro. Smoky, savory spices mingle with bright, zesty notes. A flavorful and fun handheld meal.


Procedure

    • Combine chicken, lemonade, olive oil, lime juice, and Worcestershire sauce in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in garlic powder, onion powder, and bay leaf. Simmer until chicken is tender and juices run clear, about 15–20 minutes.
    • Meanwhile, warm the tortillas in the oven or microwave until soft.
    • When the chicken is fully cooked, transfer it to a serving bowl. Place chopped tomatoes, lettuce, cheese, salsa, and sour cream in serving dishes.
    • Each person can assemble their own tacos using the tortillas and toppings.
