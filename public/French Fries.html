French Fries


Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Seasoning (optional)
  • Sauce (optional)

Description

French fries, a fast food staple and an appetizer kids love. It is a dish of salty and evenly cut strips of potatoes. It can be made in many ways, as thickness and cooking methods can vary.


Procedure

  1. Peel the potatoes
  2. Cut the potatoes into any way you want.
  3. Keep them in water to prevent browning
  4. Wash the potatoes under tap water for around 15 seconds
  5. Dry the fries under paper towels
  6. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large skillet to 300°F (165°C).
  7. Fry the potatoes for about 8 minutes
  8. Remove fries and let it cool
  9. Fry a second time in 350°F (180°C) until golden-brown.
  10. Remove fries and drain on paper towels.
  11. Season with salt or any seasoning of your choice.
  12. Toss and serve it with a sauce of your choice.
