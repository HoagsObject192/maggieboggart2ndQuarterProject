APPETIZERS


French Fries

French fries, a fast food staple and an appetizer kids love. It is a dish of salty and evenly cut strips of potatoes. It can be made in many ways, as thickness and cooking methods can vary.

Mac N' Cheese

Mac and cheese is a comfort food dish made with macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce. It is a popular side dish in American cuisine, often served as an appetizer or main course.
