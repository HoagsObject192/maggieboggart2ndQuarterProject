Chili Garlic Noodles


Ingredients

  • Wheat noodles (dried broad or egg noodles)
  • Dried chillies or chilli flakes
  • Chilli powder (Korean gochugaru or hot chilli powder)
  • Neutral oil (canola, sunflower, grapeseed, etc.)
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Black vinegar (Chingkiang vinegar)
  • Garlic, finely chopped
  • Spring onions, chopped
  • Coriander, chopped

Description

Slippery noodles are coated in a spicy, aromatic chili-garlic sauce. Chewy noodles meet bold, savory flavors in every bite. A comforting dish with a satisfying kick.


Procedure

    • Chop garlic, spring onions, and coriander.
    • Heat oil over medium-high, add garlic, sizzle 10 seconds, then lower heat.
    • Add crushed chillies and chilli powder, stir 10 seconds.
    • Turn off heat and add light soy, dark soy, sugar, sesame seeds, and black vinegar; combine well.
    • Cook noodles per packet instructions, drain, and place in a serving bowl.
    • Pour the chilli garlic oil over the noodles.
    • Garnish with spring onions, coriander, and toasted sesame seeds.
    • Toss everything together and serve immediately.
