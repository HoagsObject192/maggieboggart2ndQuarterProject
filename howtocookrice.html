How to cook rice


What you need

  • Rice Cooker/Pot
  • Rice Grains

Description

Rice is a basic for food. It is versatile, and in the Philippines, is eaten with anything. No meal (in the Philippines) is complete without rice.


Procedure

    If you have a rice cooker:

    • Put grains of rice in a rice cooker pot
    • Wash the rice with water, knead, and decantate the water. Repeat 3-5 times
    • Level the rice by tapping the pot onto a flat surface.
    • Measure the height of the rice by dipping your fingers all the way into the rice.
    • The point where the surface of the rice meets your finger is the height of the rice.
