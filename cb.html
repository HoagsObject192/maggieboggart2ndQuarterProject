CHOPPING BOARD BASICS


Chopping

Chopping means to casually and roughly cut food into bite sized pieces.

Dicing

Dicing means to PRECISELY and EVENLY cut the food into small, fine cubes creating a uniformed look.

Julienne

The Julienne cut means cutting the ingredient into LONG, uniform, matchstick-like strips. It is often used for salad ingredients.
