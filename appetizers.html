APPETIZERS


French Fries

French fries, a fast food staple and an appetizer kids love. It is a dish of salty and evenly cut strips of potatoes. It can be made in many ways, as thickness and cooking methods can vary. French fries also can be dipped in ketchup or other sauces.

Mac N' Cheese

Mac and cheese is a comfort food dish made with macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce. It is a known side dish in American cuisine, often served as an appetizer or main course. Mac and cheese is creamy and cheesy, and can satisfy cravings.

Caesar's Salad

Caesar's salad is a popular dish made with romaine lettuce, croutons, parmesan cheese, and a dressing made from anchovies, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil. It's fresh and crunchy, making it a great way to start a meal.
